False Confession

Overview

        In 2022, I decided to take part in a Game Jam through Northeastern University's Game Development Club. In this game jam, we were given one week to create a visual novel game surrounding the theme of "Confessions". Instead og strictly making a story game that the player just clicks through and reads, we wanted there to be some sort of interactive component. So, we decided to develop a story based off of the novel "The Great Gatsby" by F. Scott Fitzgerald, where the main character named Ratsby is being accused of a murder that he did not commit and you have to collect items in the room to prove that he is innocent.

        The Game Jam was hosted through Itch.io, so the final product of "False Confessions" can be found through the following link. Feel free to download the game and try it out to see if you are able to prove that Ratsby is innocent.
