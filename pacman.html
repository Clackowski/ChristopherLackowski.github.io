Pacman AI

Overview

        In 2023, I took a class named Introduction to Artificial Intelligence at Northeastern University where I learned a lot about the fundamentals of AI and real world examples of machine learning. Throughout the course the professor emphasized that artificial intelligence as a whole is basically just extremely difficult search problems. So, after brushing on relevant search algorithms, we were given the task to teach a pacman how to optimize its route to gain the most points. This involves prioritizing collecting all the dots, eating the ghosts when possible and staying alive, all of which are based on search algorithms.

        This project was completed using Python and involves some standard search algorithms such as Minimax and Expectimax. Additionally, this was my first AI project which involves multiple agents acting at once since there is the main player pacman and the enemy ghosts, so there is some added complexity there.
