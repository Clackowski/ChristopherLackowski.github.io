Touch Typer

Product Demo

Overview

        For my Engineering Capstone project, I was tasked with developing a device from scratch. After plenty of brainstorming, our group decided on designing and creating a set of gloves that tracks the movements of your fingers and allows you to type as if you were typing on a physical keyboard. This project was completed over the course of 6 months. After the device had been completed and tested, we wrote a technical report outlining the hardware, software and development processes.
